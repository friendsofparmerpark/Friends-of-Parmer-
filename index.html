Who We Are

Our Story

Friends of Parmer Park was formed by neighbors who care deeply about the future of this special place. We are a volunteer-led group working in partnership with Metro Parks and Nashville Parks Foundation, dedicated to the improvement, beautification, and revitalization of Parmer Park for the neighborhood today and generations to come.

Community Input

Your Feedback Matters

We are gathering community input to better understand how neighbors use Parmer Park and what improvements would have the greatest impact. Your voice shapes the future of this park.
